# FREEDOLIAPP V1 — Navigation Map

## 1. Purpose

FREEDOLIAPP V1 needs a **single, explicit navigation map** so that routing, menus, and UX structure stay coherent as the product grows.  
This document is the **source-of-truth** for which routes exist, how they are grouped, and which ones are eligible to appear in primary navigation (sidebar/topbar) for V1.

---

## 2. Navigation principles

- **Public vs authenticated boundary**
  - All **public** routes live **outside** `/app`.
  - All **authenticated product** routes live **inside** `/app`.
- **Legal routes are always public**
  - `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/cookies`, `/dpa`, `/legal` must remain public and accessible without login.
- **Clarity over feature sprawl**
  - Primary navigation should favor clear, stable destinations over exposing every feature surface.
- **Sidebar for core product areas only**
  - The sidebar should list only enduring, high-value product sections (operations, intelligence, system/account).
- **Utility/system routes are not auto-primary**
  - Debug/admin, transitional setup flows, and narrow utility pages **do not** automatically become sidebar items.
- **Deep links and wizards sit under parents**
  - Detail views, wizards, and locked/edge states are addressed as contextual routes, not as top-level destinations.

## 3. Public navigation

Public-facing routes (no authentication required):

- **`/`**
  - **Purpose:** Marketing/landing page and primary entrypoint to the product.  
  - **Audience:** New visitors, prospects, returning users who are not yet logged in.  
  - **Placement:** Header + direct link (main entry), plus footer.

- **`/login`**
  - **Purpose:** Authentication surface for email/password and magic-link login; trial lead capture.  
  - **Audience:** Prospects starting a trial, returning users.  
  - **Placement:** Header (e.g. “Log in” button) and from landing CTAs; not in footer as a legal/info link.

- **`/legal`**
  - **Purpose:** Legal hub listing main legal documents.  
  - **Audience:** All visitors, customers, legal/compliance reviewers.  
  - **Placement:** Footer link; may also be referenced from account/help pages.

- **`/privacy`**
  - **Purpose:** Privacy Policy display.  
  - **Audience:** All visitors, especially at signup and during due diligence.  
  - **Placement:** Footer link, legal hub, and linked from signup/login consent text.

- **`/terms`**
  - **Purpose:** Terms of Service display.  
  - **Audience:** All visitors; required reading at account creation.  
  - **Placement:** Footer link, legal hub, and linked from signup/login consent text.

- **`/cookies`**
  - **Purpose:** Cookie Policy display.  
  - **Audience:** All visitors, particularly within the EU/EEA.  
  - **Placement:** Footer link, legal hub, and referenced from cookie banner / preferences.

- **`/dpa`**
  - **Purpose:** Data Processing Agreement information.  
  - **Audience:** B2B customers, legal/compliance teams.  
  - **Placement:** Footer link, legal hub, and referenced from billing/account pages.

## 4. Authenticated V1 application navigation (`/app`)

All main product routes live under `/app`. Below are the core destinations for V1, grouped by area.

### Core Operations

- **`/app` → Dashboard**
  - **Purpose:** High-level overview (KPIs, tasks, alerts) for daily operations.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes (as “Dashboard”).  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/projects`**
  - **Purpose:** Project-based view of products/initiatives (central operational unit).  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/suppliers`**
  - **Purpose:** Manage suppliers and related operational data.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/orders`**
  - **Purpose:** Purchase order management and order-linked operations.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/inventory`**
  - **Purpose:** Inventory views and stock signals relevant to ordering and risk.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/finances`**
  - **Purpose:** Financials overview (profit, cashflow, key financial metrics).  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1 (some sub-capabilities may be partial).

### Intelligence

- **`/app/decisions`**
  - **Purpose:** Decision inbox/list — central view of decisions generated by the engine.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/automations`**
  - **Purpose:** Automation Inbox for reviewing automation proposals and their status.  
  - **Sidebar:** yes.  
  - **Priority:** primary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/automations/activity`**
  - **Purpose:** Automation activity timeline (events across proposals and executions).  
  - **Sidebar:** optional (can be accessible via Automation area or sub-navigation).  
  - **Priority:** secondary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/automations/analytics`**
  - **Purpose:** Automation analytics dashboard (metrics, funnel, risk, reliability, velocity).  
  - **Sidebar:** optional (may appear as sub-nav item under Automation or via Analytics section).  
  - **Priority:** secondary.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

### System / Account

- **`/app/billing`**
  - **Purpose:** Workspace billing settings (plans, subscription state, Stripe portal links).  
  - **Sidebar:** no (access via account/workspace/top navigation).  
  - **Priority:** utility.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1.

- **`/app/settings`**
  - **Purpose:** Workspace and user-level settings (depending on current implementation).  
  - **Sidebar:** no (exposed via top navigation / user menu).  
  - **Priority:** utility.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1 (some sub-settings may be partial).

- **Other account/workspace routes (e.g. `/app/billing/locked`, `/app/billing/over-seat`)**  
  - **Purpose:** Gating states related to billing/seat limits.  
  - **Sidebar:** no.  
  - **Priority:** utility/edge.  
  - **V1 Status:** in V1 as contextual routes.

## 5. Detail and child routes

The following routes are part of the product but are **not** primary navigation items. They are reached via actions, deep links, or contextual navigation:

- **Project detail and variations**
  - `\`/app/projects/:id\`` — project detail views.  
  - `\`/app/projects/:projectId/briefing\`` — project briefing context.  
  - **Role:** Layer C (detail / by-action).

- **Automation proposal detail**
  - `\`/app/automations/:proposalId\`` — automation proposal detail, approvals, execution panel.  
  - **Role:** Layer C.

- **Billing locked / over-seat**
  - `\`/app/billing/locked\``, `\`/app/billing/over-seat\`` — billing/seat gating states.  
  - **Role:** Layer C; never primary destinations.

- **Activation wizard**
  - `/activation` — onboarding/ActivationWizard flow for new workspaces.  
  - **Role:** Transitional setup; Layer C (accessed automatically or via onboarding logic, not main nav).

- **Amazon-specific setup/imports**
  - Examples: `\`/app/finances/amazon-imports\`` or similar detail views.  
  - **Role:** Detail/utilities under Finances/Operations; Layer C.

Other detail routes (diagnostics, admin, dev seed, calendar sub-views, etc.) are also considered **Layer C** unless explicitly promoted.

## 6. Navigation layers

FREEDOLIAPP V1 distinguishes three navigation layers:

### Layer A — Public site

- Landing, login, and legal pages: `/`, `/login`, `/legal`, `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/cookies`, `/dpa`.  
- Accessible without authentication; focused on acquisition, explanation, and legal compliance.

### Layer B — Main product navigation

- Core operational destinations surfaced in sidebar/topbar:  
  - `/app` (Dashboard), `/app/projects`, `/app/suppliers`, `/app/orders`, `/app/inventory`, `/app/finances`  
  - `/app/decisions`, `/app/automations`, potentially `/app/automations/activity` and `/app/automations/analytics` as part of “Intelligence” navigation.

### Layer C — Contextual/detail/navigation-by-action routes

- Deep links, detail pages, wizard steps, locked/gating states, support/diagnostics.  
- Entered from Layer B destinations or via direct links, but not shown as first-level navigation in V1.

---

## 7. Explicit exclusions from V1 primary navigation

The following areas/routes/systems **must not** be shown as primary sidebar items in V1:

- Future commerce connectors:
  - Shopify-related routes (e.g. `/app/shopify/*`)  
  - eBay or other marketplaces
- Future systems:
  - Listing Intelligence surfaces (draft builder, keyword tools)  
  - Assistant/copilot entrypoints
- Internal/technical:
  - Internal diagnostics beyond `/app/diagnostics` as a utility.  
  - Admin/debug consoles not meant for standard operators.  
  - Developer-only tools like `/app/dev/seed`.
- Transitional setup flows:
  - One-off activation or migration flows that are not stable, repeatable product areas.

These routes may exist and be accessible, but they must **not** be elevated to primary navigation without revisiting this document.

## 8. Recommended sidebar structure for V1

A concise, V1-focused sidebar grouping:

- **Dashboard**
  - `/app` (Dashboard)
- **Operations**
  - `/app/projects`
  - `/app/orders`
  - `/app/suppliers`
  - `/app/inventory`
  - `/app/finances`
- **Intelligence**
  - `/app/decisions`
  - `/app/automations`
  - Optionally sub-links or internal tabs for `/app/automations/activity` and `/app/automations/analytics`.

All other destinations (settings, billing, diagnostics, help) should be reached via top navigation, user menu, or contextual links.

---

## 9. Top navigation / utility navigation

Items that belong in the **top navigation or user menu**, not in the sidebar:

- **Workspace switcher**
  - Selecting active workspace/org.  
  - May expose shortcut to workspace-related settings.

- **Billing / account**
  - Link to `/app/billing` from account or workspace menu.  
  - Not a primary sidebar item.

- **Profile / logout**
  - User avatar, profile access, language/preferences, logout.  
  - Top-right user menu only.

- **Help / support**
  - Access to help documentation, support contact, or diagnostics entry.  
  - Top navigation or footer; not a primary sidebar group.

- **Legal links (optional)**
  - Additional references to `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/cookies`, `/dpa` from footer or help section; primary legal access remains via public footer and `/legal`.

## 11. Source-of-truth rule

This document defines the **official FREEDOLIAPP V1 navigation structure**.  

- New routes **must not** automatically appear in primary navigation (sidebar or topbar) unless they are:  
  - added to this document, or  
  - explicitly approved and reflected here in a follow-up update.  
- When in doubt, routes should default to **Layer C (contextual/detail)** and be reachable from existing Layer B destinations rather than creating new primary navigation items.

Future phases (post-V1) may introduce new sections (e.g., Listing Intelligence, Shopify), but they must extend this map deliberately, not ad hoc.
