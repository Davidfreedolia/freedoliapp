# FREEDOLIAPP — PRODUCT POSITIONING

Status: Draft  
Owner: Product  
Scope: Product definition (not UI, not marketing implementation)

---

## Product Category

Freedoliapp is an **AI-assisted operations platform for Amazon sellers**.

It helps sellers manage the operational side of their business:

- inventory
- purchase orders
- suppliers
- shipments
- financial visibility
- operational decisions

The goal is to transform fragmented seller workflows into a **single operational control system**.

---

## Target Users

Primary:

Small and medium Amazon sellers that:

- import products
- manage suppliers
- send inventory to FBA
- operate across multiple marketplaces

Typical user profile:

- 1–20 active products
- sourcing from China / EU
- shipping to Amazon warehouses

---

## Core Value Proposition

Freedoliapp allows sellers to:

- see the **real state of their business**
- detect **operational risks early**
- receive **clear decisions about what to do next**

Instead of spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

---

## Product Philosophy

Freedoliapp is not a generic analytics dashboard.

It is a **decision-driven operations system**.

The product focuses on:

- clarity
- actionable information
- operational control

---

## Key Product Concept

The central idea of Freedoliapp is:

> **A control tower for Amazon sellers.**

The system shows:

- what is happening
- what is wrong
- what action should be taken

---

## Future Expansion

While Amazon is the primary integration, the system architecture allows expansion to:

- Shopify
- other marketplaces
- supplier and logistics integrations
