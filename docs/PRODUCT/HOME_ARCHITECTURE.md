# FREEDOLIAPP — HOME ARCHITECTURE

Status: Draft  
Scope: Application home screen structure

---

## Purpose of the Home

The home page acts as the **control center of the business**.

It should allow the user to understand the situation in seconds.

The page answers three questions:

1. How is the business performing?
2. Are there problems?
3. What needs attention?

---

## Structure

The home page is divided into four main sections.

---

## 1 — Business Status

A compact overview of the current situation.

Example metrics:

- profit
- revenue
- inventory health
- active products

The goal is quick situational awareness.

---

## 2 — Decision Feed

The decision feed highlights important operational signals.

Examples:

- reorder required
- margin drop
- opportunity signals

Each item should be:

- clear
- actionable
- linked to the relevant product or operation.

---

## 3 — Operations Overview

A quick summary of operational activity.

Examples:

- purchase orders
- shipments
- suppliers
- projects

---

## 4 — Products

A visual list of the user's products or projects.

Each item may include:

- thumbnail
- product name
- stock status
- operational progress

---

## Design Principle

The home page must prioritize:

- clarity
- speed of understanding
- actionable information

Avoid:

- complex analytics
- large tables
- dense data.
