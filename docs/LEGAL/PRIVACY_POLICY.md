# FREEDOLIAPP — PRIVACY POLICY

Status: Draft  
Owner: Legal / Product  
Scope: GDPR privacy notice for FREEDOLIAPP

## 1 — Introduction

FREEDOLIAPP (“Freedoliapp”, “we”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

This Privacy Policy explains how we process personal data when users access and use the Freedoliapp platform.

---

## 2 — Data Collected

We process the following categories of data:

**User account data**  
Data required to create and manage user accounts, such as:

- name  
- email address  
- workspace or organization membership  
- login metadata

**Organization data**  
Data related to the company using the platform, such as:

- organization name  
- subscription plan  
- workspace configuration and settings

**Operational data**  
Data required to operate the platform’s core functionality, including:

- suppliers  
- purchase orders  
- inventory and stock levels  
- related operational decisions

Freedoliapp does **not** process or store customer payment card data. Payments are handled by **Stripe**.

---

## 3 — Purpose of Processing

We process personal and operational data for the following purposes:

- **Provide services**: to operate the platform and deliver its core features (supplier management, purchase orders, inventory and operational visibility).  
- **Maintain accounts**: to create, authenticate and administer user and organization accounts.  
- **Improve platform functionality**: to monitor usage, enhance performance, improve features and maintain security of the service.

---

## 4 — Data Storage

Data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure providers, including:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, APIs and storage.  
- **Vercel** — hosting of the web application and frontend assets.

These providers implement industry-standard security measures and encryption.

---

## 5 — Subprocessors

For the provision of the service, Freedoliapp relies on the following subprocessors:

- **Supabase** — data hosting, authentication and APIs.  
- **Vercel** — application and frontend hosting.  
- **Stripe** — subscription billing and payment processing.

Each subprocessor maintains its own security and compliance certifications.

---

## 6 — Data Retention

We retain personal and operational data for as long as the customer account remains active and as necessary to provide the service.

Upon account termination, data may be deleted or anonymized in accordance with our internal retention policies. Backups may temporarily retain copies for a limited period as part of standard backup procedures.

---

## 7 — User Rights

Under GDPR, users may have the following rights in relation to their personal data:

- **Right of access**: request information about the personal data we hold.  
- **Right to rectification**: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.  
- **Right to erasure**: request deletion of personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

Users can exercise these rights by contacting us using the contact details below. We may need to verify identity before fulfilling requests.
