# FREEDOLIAPP — Cookie Policy (Stub)

Status: Draft (structure only, not final legal text)  

## Purpose

Explain which cookies and similar technologies FREEDOLIAPP uses, for what purposes, and how users can manage their preferences in a GDPR-compliant way.

## Key sections to be completed

- Types of cookies used (essential, analytics, functional, possibly marketing)  
- Detailed list or categories of cookies/technologies and their purposes  
- Legal basis for setting non-essential cookies (consent)  
- How users can **accept, reject, and later change** cookie preferences  
- Interaction with browser settings and do-not-track signals (if applicable)  
- Retention periods and expiry of cookies  
- Links to Privacy Policy and relevant settings pages

## FREEDOLIAPP-specific notes

- Essential cookies are required for Supabase auth sessions and basic app security.  
- Analytics cookies (e.g. product analytics tools) must **not** be set until the user has explicitly consented.  
- The product must offer a real choice, not just an “Accept all” banner, and a way to reopen cookie settings.
