# FREEDOLIAPP — Acceptable Use Policy (Stub)

Status: Draft (structure only, not final legal text)  

## Purpose

Set clear rules about how FREEDOLIAPP may and may not be used, to protect the platform, other customers, and third parties from abuse or illegal activity.

## Key sections to be completed

- Prohibited activities (abuse, security attacks, data scraping, fraud, harassment, spam)  
- Misuse of Amazon or other marketplace data (e.g. violating API terms or policies)  
- Use of automation features in ways that break third-party terms or laws  
- Responsibilities of workspace owners vs members  
- Enforcement measures (warnings, suspension, termination)  
- Reporting channels for abuse or security issues  
- Relationship with Terms of Service (AUP as part of or referenced by the Terms)

## FREEDOLIAPP-specific notes

- Users must not use FREEDOLIAPP to **circumvent Amazon policies**, scrape data outside official APIs, or attempt unauthorized access to any systems.  
- Automation features must be configured and used in a way that respects marketplace rules and local laws; FREEDOLIAPP does not endorse or support abusive automations.  
- FREEDOLIAPP reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate this policy to protect the platform and its customers.
