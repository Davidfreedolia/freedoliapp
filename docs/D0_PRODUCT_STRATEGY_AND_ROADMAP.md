# FREEDOLIAPP — PRODUCT STRATEGY & ROADMAP

## Vision

Freedoliapp is designed to become:

**"Amazon Seller Operating System"**

A unified platform where Amazon sellers manage:

- product development
- supplier sourcing
- launch pipeline
- financial truth
- portfolio intelligence

---

## Product Philosophy

Freedoliapp focuses on four core pillars:

1. **Product Brain**
2. **Profit Truth Engine**
3. **Launch Pipeline**
4. **Portfolio Brain**

These pillars transform the platform from a tool into an operating system.

## F10 PRODUCT CORE (Strategic Layer)

This layer contains the competitive advantage of Freedoliapp.

### F10.1 Product Brain

Per-product intelligence layer containing:

- market context
- cost structure
- supplier pricing
- unit economics
- performance data

**Purpose:** Connect product research, supplier sourcing and real performance.

No external APIs required for V1.

---

### F10.2 Profit Truth Engine

Financial truth engine calculating:

- real profit
- margin
- ROI
- cash impact

**Data sources:**

- financial_ledger
- supplier costs
- shipments
- Amazon settlements

This becomes the primary value driver of the platform.

---

### F10.3 Launch Pipeline Engine

Automation layer for product launches.

**Pipeline stages:**

- Idea
- Viability
- Quotes
- Samples
- Purchase Order
- Shipment
- Launch

**Examples of automation:**

- quote accepted → suggest sample request
- sample approved → suggest purchase order
- shipment delivered → ready to launch

---

### F10.4 Portfolio Brain

Portfolio-level intelligence.

**Dashboard insights:**

- top performing products
- worst performing products
- margin erosion
- stockout risk
- dead inventory risk

**Purpose:** Help sellers manage large product portfolios.

---

## UX Completion Before Beta

Before releasing to real users the following areas must be polished.

| Area | Focus |
|------|--------|
| **Wizard** | simple onboarding, Amazon connection, quick activation |
| **Landing** | clear value proposition, Amazon seller focus |
| **Dashboard** | minimal but informative, snapshot + actions |
| **Projects** | visual portfolio, launch pipeline clarity |
| **Project Detail** | clear pipeline, actionable next steps |
| **Snapshot** | immediate financial insight |

---

## Beta Strategy

**Initial beta group:** 5–10 Amazon sellers.

**Objectives:**

- validate usability
- detect confusion
- refine workflow

**Beta users receive:** 3 months free access in exchange for feedback.

---
