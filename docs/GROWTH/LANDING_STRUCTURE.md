# FREEDOLIAPP — LANDING STRUCTURE

Status: Draft  
Scope: Marketing entry point

---

## Purpose

The landing page exists to convert visitors into **trial users**.

The page must clearly communicate:

- what Freedoliapp does
- why it matters
- why it is different

---

## Core Message

Freedoliapp helps sellers **run their business with clarity and control**.

The product is not just analytics.

It is an **operational system for sellers**.

---

## Landing Funnel


Visitor
→ Landing page
→ Trial form
→ Magic link login
→ Activation
→ Application


---

## Landing Sections

### 1 — Hero

Goal: explain the product immediately.

Structure:

Headline  
Short explanation  
Primary CTA

CTA:

Start free trial

---

### 2 — Problem

Explain current seller pain:

- spreadsheets
- fragmented tools
- operational chaos

---

### 3 — Solution

Freedoliapp as the control system.

Key ideas:

- clarity
- operational visibility
- decision support

---

### 4 — Product Overview

Visual explanation of the main product surfaces:

- decisions
- inventory
- operations
- suppliers
- finances

---

### 5 — CTA

Second strong CTA to start the trial.

---

## Important Principles

Landing must be:

- simple
- focused
- clear

Avoid:

- technical explanations
- complex charts
- deep product detail
